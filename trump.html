CLIMATE DENIALDIRECT
US withdraws from Paris Agreement; 100+ EPA regulations rolled back
Trump had called climate change a Chinese hoax repeatedly from 2012 onwards. June 1, 2017: withdrawal from the Paris Climate Agreement announced. The mechanism mirrors the film: tribal and corporate interests overriding documented warnings already in progress.

THE SYSTEMIC QUESTIONLOOSE
Trump lost in 2020, was convicted in 2024, and won again anyway
The film's most honest prediction isn't any specific gag — it's the structural thesis. Trump lost the 2020 election, was impeached twice, was convicted on 34 felony counts, and was re-elected by a clear margin in November 2024. The Idiocracy point isn't that bad leaders win. It's that the systems producing them persist after any individual is removed. The pimp's pod always opens.
