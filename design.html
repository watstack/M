Shape & shadow

Everything is --radius: 2px (near-square, 8-bit) with a beveled inset highlight + a hard offset drop shadow (Npx Npx 0 rgba(0,0,0,.5)) instead of blur — this is the single most defining trait of the system. Buttons additionally "press in" on hover/active by translating toward the shadow.
