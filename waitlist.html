FAQ

Got questions?

Which city are you launching in first?▾
London is first. We're targeting a launch later this year with a curated set of venues across zones 1–3. Other UK cities — Manchester, Bristol, Edinburgh — are next.
How much will it cost?▾
We're not publishing the final price yet — but the ballpark is £49–£59/month. Founding members (the first 500) get a locked-in rate below that, for life.
Is this for playing sport, watching sport, or both?▾
Both — that's the point. Your M pass covers bookable sessions (padel, climbing, five-a-side, tennis, run clubs etc.) AND tickets to local live sporting events. One membership, both sides of the experience.
Is there a long-term contract?▾
No. M is month-to-month — cancel any time, no penalty, no awkward phone call.
How does the Golden Ticket draw work?▾
Everyone gets at least 1 entry. Earn more by referring friends — each signup via your link = +1 entry. At launch, one winner is drawn at random, weighted by entries, and gets a full M pass for 12 months completely free.
When do you launch?▾
We're targeting later this year for London. Joining the waitlist guarantees you're first to know — and first through the door.
Do I need to be athletic?▾
Not at all. Sessions range from casual social play to competitive leagues. M is for anyone who wants more sport in their life — at whatever level.
