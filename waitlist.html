FAQ

Questions.

What is M?
M is a single membership that covers live sport across Melbourne — AFL, A-League football, NBL basketball, Super Netball, NRL and more. One pass, every code, no lock-in.
How much will it cost?
Ballpark is $59–$79/month AUD. Founding members — the first 500 — get a locked-in rate below that, for life.
How does the Golden Ticket draw work?
Everyone on the waitlist gets 1 entry. Each friend who joins via your link = +1 entry. At launch one winner is drawn at random and gets a full M membership for 12 months, completely free.
When do you launch?
Melbourne is first — later this year. Joining now guarantees you're first to know, and first through the door.
