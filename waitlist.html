FAQ

Questions.

Which city are you launching in first?
Melbourne is first — the world capital of sport. We're targeting a launch later this year across the inner suburbs and CBD. Sydney and Brisbane are next.
How much will it cost?
We're not publishing the final price yet — but the ballpark is $59–$79/month AUD. Founding members (the first 500) get a locked-in rate below that, for life.
Is this for playing sport, watching sport, or both?
Both — that's the point. Your M pass covers bookable sessions (padel, climbing, five-a-side, tennis, run clubs etc.) AND tickets to live local sporting events. One membership, both sides.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. M is month-to-month — cancel any time, no penalty, no awkward phone call.
How does the Golden Ticket draw work?
Everyone on the waitlist gets at least 1 entry. Earn more by referring friends — each signup via your link = +1 entry. At launch, one winner is drawn at random, weighted by entries, and gets a full M pass for 12 months, completely free.
When do you launch?
We're targeting later this year for Melbourne. Joining the waitlist guarantees you're first to know — and first through the door. We'll send an update 2 weeks before launch.
Do I need to be athletic?
Not at all. Sessions range from casual social play to competitive leagues. M is for anyone who wants more sport in their life — at whatever level.
