# Job Scout Criteria

## Target Roles

- Senior Product Manager
- Lead Product Manager
- Principal Product Manager
- Staff Product Manager
- Head of Product
- Group Product Manager

## Location

**Accepted:**
- Melbourne (hybrid OK; max 3 days per week in office)
- Remote — Australia-based hiring or meaningful AEST overlap (UTC+10/+11)

**Excluded:**
- Relocation required
- Sydney-only hybrid (i.e. no remote option and office is Sydney)

## Company Profile

- **Preferred:** B2B SaaS
- **Strong signal:** Product-led growth (PLG) model
- **Stage:** Scale-up / growth stage (Series A–D, or equivalent headcount ~50–500)
- **Strong signal:** AI / automation-adjacent product surface

## Compensation

- **Floor:** $[X]K AUD base ← _fill this in before first scout run_
- Salary transparency (range published in listing) is a positive ranking signal
- Equity/options noted but not required

## Dealbreakers

- Agencies or consultancies
- Contract roles under 12 months
- Roles that are purely execution/delivery PM with no strategic scope
- Relocation required

## Ranking Signals (strong → weak)

1. **Staff / Head-of scope** — leads multiple product squads or works across 10+ engineers
2. **AI / ML product surface** — the product itself involves AI, ML, or automation
3. **Remote-first with AU presence** — company is remote-first and has AU-based team or entity
4. **Salary transparency** — range is published in the listing
5. **PLG company** — product is the primary growth channel
6. **B2B SaaS** — software sold to businesses on subscription

## Notes for CV Matching

- Favour roles where scope matches a background in cross-functional product leadership
- Weight AI/automation-adjacent roles higher if the CV shows relevant domain experience
- A listed salary below the floor is an automatic skip, even if other signals are strong
